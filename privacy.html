Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Overview

This application is a personal-use integration that connects to QuickBooks Online via the Intuit API. It is operated solely by Daniel Santana for his own internal bookkeeping and financial management purposes. No data is shared with third parties.

2. Data We Access

By authorizing this application, it may access the following data from your QuickBooks Online account:

  • Company information (name, address, fiscal year)
  • Customers and vendors
  • Invoices, bills, payments, and expenses
  • Chart of accounts and financial reports

3. How We Use Your Data

All data accessed through this application is used exclusively for personal financial management and bookkeeping. Data is processed locally on the user's own machine and is never transmitted to external servers or third-party services.

4. Data Storage

OAuth tokens required for authentication are stored locally on the user's machine at ~/.qbo/tokens.json. No QuickBooks data is stored persistently — all data is fetched on demand and held only in memory during a session.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. This application has no analytics, advertising, or data collection components.

6. Security

Access tokens are stored locally and protected by the security controls of the user's operating system. The application uses OAuth 2.0 for authentication with Intuit's platform.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke this application's access to your QuickBooks Online account at any time via accounts.intuit.com under Connected Apps.

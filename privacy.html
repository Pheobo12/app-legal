Privacy Policy

Pie Bookkeeper · Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Overview

Pie Bookkeeper is internal software owned and operated by Pie Accounting, LLC ("Pie Accounting," "we," "us," or "our"). It is used exclusively by Pie Accounting's authorized bookkeeping staff to deliver bookkeeping and accounting services to Pie Accounting's clients. Pie Bookkeeper is not made available to the public, to clients directly, or to any other firm. This policy explains what data Pie Bookkeeper accesses through the QuickBooks Online API, how that data is used, and how it is protected.

2. Data We Access

Under its existing engagement to provide bookkeeping services, Pie Accounting connects Pie Bookkeeper to the QuickBooks Online company files of its clients via the Intuit API. Depending on the services provided to a given client, this may include:

  • Company information (name, address, fiscal year, chart of accounts)
  • Customers and vendors
  • Invoices, bills, payments, and expense transactions
  • Bank and credit card transaction feeds used for reconciliation
  • Financial reports (profit & loss, balance sheet, aging summaries)

3. How We Use Data

Data accessed through Pie Bookkeeper is used exclusively to perform bookkeeping and accounting services on behalf of Pie Accounting's clients — for example, categorizing transactions, reconciling accounts, running month-end close checklists, and following up with clients about unresolved items. Access is limited to the bookkeeping staff assigned to a given client. Data is never used for advertising, sold, or repurposed for any use unrelated to the client engagement under which it was collected.

4. Data Storage and Security

Pie Bookkeeper connects to QuickBooks Online over encrypted (TLS/HTTPS) connections, consistent with Intuit's API requirements. Each user of Pie Bookkeeper authenticates with individual, per-user credentials, and access to client data is restricted to the staff assigned to that client. Pie Accounting maintains a Written Information Security Program (WISP) governing safeguards for client financial data — including multi-factor authentication on firm systems, encryption, access controls, and vendor oversight — consistent with IRS Publication 4557 and the FTC Safeguards Rule. OAuth credentials used to connect to QuickBooks Online are held in a secured credential store and are never shared outside Pie Accounting.

5. Data Sharing

Pie Accounting does not sell, rent, or share client data accessed through Pie Bookkeeper with third parties, except as necessary to provide the bookkeeping services a client has engaged Pie Accounting to perform (for example, a service provider supporting Pie Accounting's operations under its own confidentiality obligations). Pie Bookkeeper has no advertising or analytics components that transmit client data to outside parties.

6. Data Retention

Client data is retained only as long as needed to provide bookkeeping services and to meet Pie Accounting's recordkeeping obligations under applicable law and its client engagement agreements. Clients may request deletion of data specific to Pie Bookkeeper by contacting Pie Accounting directly.

7. Your Rights

Clients and Pie Accounting personnel may revoke Pie Bookkeeper's access to a QuickBooks Online company at any time via accounts.intuit.com under Connected Apps. Questions about data handling can be directed to the contact below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Pie Bookkeeper's features or hosting change. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, contact: accountant@piebooking.com
